Our school

Masai International Academy is a group of schools including a Creche, Nursery, Primary, Junior and Secondary School, located at Maitama zone c, FCT Abuja Nigeria.

The creche, Nursery and Primary school was founded in year 2000 and barely two years afterwards the Junior Secondary was established and upgraded to a Senior Secondary School after the first JSCE in June 2005.
Afterwhich the University was built in year 2010, in Califonia USA
